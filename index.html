About

I joined Cohere in the next-generation agents team as a Member of Technical Staff in January 2026, focusing on building smarter agents powered by LLMs for complex tasks.

I submitted my PhD (2025) in NLP at the University of Edinburgh through the UKRI CDT programme, supervised by Alex Lascarides and Frank Keller.

My research explores the intersection of Multimodal Learning, Interactive Learning, and Representation Learning.
